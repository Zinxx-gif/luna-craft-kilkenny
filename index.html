Our Story

Made with Magic,
Made in Kilkenny

LunaCraft Kilkenny is a small, independent Irish brand creating handmade witchy and spiritual home decor from the heart of County Kilkenny. Every piece is crafted by hand with care and intention, designed to bring a sense of calm, magic, and meaning to your everyday spaces.

From crystal infused candles to eco resin trinket trays and hand-crafted spell jars, each item is shaped with a deep love for spiritual living. No mass production, no shortcuts -- just genuine craft and the belief that your home should feel sacred.
